DNA Databases and Privacy Concerns

Resource 1

URLFBI CODIS Facts
AuthorFederal Bureau of Investigation
AbstractThis fact sheet explains how CODIS (Combined DNA Index System) works. It describes how DNA is collected, stored, and used in solving crimes. The document explains the legal use of the system and who can be entered into it. While it shows the benefits, it also raises questions about who should be included and the risks of misuse or privacy violations.

AuthorWired Staff
AbstractThis article looks into how law enforcement uses genealogy databases to investigate suspects. It raises concerns about people unknowingly exposing relatives to criminal investigations. The piece discusses how companies handle user data and the lack of clear legal protections. It stresses the importance of stronger privacy laws as DNA databases grow.
